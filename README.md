# 🚀 Project Highlights

- 📊 Analyzed **9,994 retail sales transactions** from the Superstore Sales dataset.
- 💰 Examined over **$2.30M USD** in sales and **$286.3K USD** in profit to evaluate overall business performance.
- 🗂️ Processed **23 business attributes**, including Sales, Profit, Discount, Customer, Product, Region, Category, and Shipping details.
- 🧹 Cleaned and transformed raw data by handling missing values, removing invalid records, converting currency and percentage fields into numerical format, and formatting date columns.
- 📈 Conducted **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** using **Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and Seaborn** to uncover meaningful business insights.
- 🌍 Compared sales performance across **4 regions**, **50 states**, and **3 product categories**.
- 📉 Evaluated the relationship between **discounts and profitability** to identify opportunities for improving business performance.
- 📦 Identified top-performing categories, sub-categories, products, and regions using data-driven analysis.
- 📓 Developed entirely in **Python using Jupyter Notebook**, following an end-to-end data analytics workflow.
